Bootstrap’s powerful, responsive navigation header, the Navbar https://getbootstrap.com/docs/4.1/components/navbar/ https://medium.com/wdstack/examples-bootstrap-4-navbar-b3c9dc0edc1a Now the Navbar v4 has 6 breakpoint sizes or “states” using the new navbar-expand-* classes -- The Navbar never collapses into the vertical mobile view, and is always horizontal. . navbar-expand = never collapses vertically (remains horizontal) -- The Navbar is always collapsed into the vertical view, and toggled via the hamburger icon. . remove navbar-expand-* -- The Navbar expands into horizontal mode at one of the 4 responsive breakpoints. . navbar-expand-sm = collapses below sm widths <576px . navbar-expand-md = collapses below md widths <768px . navbar-expand-lg = collapses below lg widths <992px . navbar-expand-xl = collapses below xl widths <1200px Navbar Color or Transparency and Color of the hamburger icon https://medium.com/wdstack/bootstrap-4-custom-navbar-1f6a2da5ed3c -- Background color using the bg-* classes . bg-light, bg-dark, bg-info, bg-danger, bg-primary, bg-warning, or bg-success . navbar-dark for lighter text color (white) . navbar-light for darker text color (black)
